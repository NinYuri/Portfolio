EXPERIENCE

Here’s a look into the academic journey that took me beyond the classroom — where I collaborated with international researchers, expanded my perspective and applied my skills in a meaningful project.

Dolphin

RESEARCH PROGRAM 2024

An Escape to the Past: Immersive Narratives of Pioneer Women


A multiplayer VR escape room that blends storytelling, education and interactive design. Focused on Ada Lovelace, the experience takes players through themed rooms — like a dungeon, library and bedroom — each filled with puzzles inspired by her life and legacy.


This project involved historial research, conceptual design, Unity development and multiplayer integration using C#. It was created during a research internship in collaboration with Colombian researchers as part of the XXIX Delfín Summer Program.

After completing the project, I had the honor of presenting it at the International Research Congress of the XXIX Dolphin Summer Program, held from August 28 to 31, 2024.

As a recognized speaker, I shared our development process, research insights and creative challenges with a diverse audience of students, researchers and professionals from across Latin America


It was a rewarding experience that not only celebrated our work, but also strengthened my skills in public speaking, academic communication, and intercultural collaboration.
